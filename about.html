About Expats

Expats has created a unique user experience. Don't worry about the hassle of limited character counts or the inability to post images and your travel stories together -- sign up with us and do both!

Share all your adventures with those who can't come along with you. Update pictures in real time along with your stories, the people you meet or places you go. You never know who you could inspire to become a fellow expat!

With future updates you'll not only be able to upload just images, but your videos as well! Look forward to other fun features such as location tagging, user and location searches, and even an onboard photo editor!
